Instructions

Try to translate the given sentence in your head, one word at a time. After guessing a word, click on the blank ______ to show the word and see if you were correct.

If you get a word wrong, click on it again to hide it. You can try to remember it again at the end.

Click on Show all to show all the words.

Click on Reset to hide all the words.

Click on Show hints to turn the hints on. A hint is the first letter of the word and its length.

Do you want to repeat this sentence later? If so, leave some or all the words hidden and come back to it later. If not, leave all the words showing.

Click on -> to go to the next sentence.

Jump around between sentences by clicking on the sentence numbers like 1 2 3 above.

Your progress on each sentence is tracked above. Completed sentences turn green, and uncompleted sentences remain red.
