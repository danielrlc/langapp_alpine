Instructions

Translate the given English sentence into Finnish in your head. After translating a word, click on the blank ______ to see if you were correct.

If you get a word wrong, click on it again to hide it and try again later.

Click on Show all to show all the words. Click again to hide.

Click on Show hints to turn hints on. A hint is the first letter of the word and its length. Click again to hide.

Do you want to repeat this sentence later? If so, leave some or all the words hidden and come back to it later. If not, leave all the words showing.

Click on -> to go to the next sentence, or jump around between sentences by clicking on the sentence numbers like 1 2 3 below.

Your progress on each sentence is tracked below. Completed sentences turn green, and uncompleted sentences are red (not learnt) or yellow (half learnt).
